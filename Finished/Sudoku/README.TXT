Name: Sudoku
 
Author: Simon Kaudelka 
 
Description:
A 9x9 Sudoku

Click on the field you want to select.
Selected fields will be highligted.
Once you selected a field, either press '0' to delet the number
or the keys '1' to '9' to insert the respective number.

Press 'N' to create a new Sudoku.

Press 'C' to check, if your Solution is correct.

A executable .jar is not available because the game uses the console
